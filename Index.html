WAYNE ENTERPRISES

THE WAYNE TOWER

The base operations of Wayne Enterprises, and the billionare Bruce Wayne himself. Wayne Tower was built in 1888 by the first Wayne Brothers. In 2002, Thomas Wayne moved himself, his wife Martha, and their son Bruce, to Wayne Tower during the former's mayoral election.

GOTHAM CITY

Gotham City is a bustling, dark city located in the U.S. state of New Jersey. Gotham is rife with corruption, poverty, vermin infestations, pollution, and numerous crime waves. The city's motto is "Sic Parvis Magna."
